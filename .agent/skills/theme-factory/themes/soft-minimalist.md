# Soft Minimalist

A minimalist and soft theme designed to break away from standard framework defaults (like Tailwind CSS). It combines a warm greige foundation, muted Morandi accents, and humanistic typography to build a clean yet gentle visual experience.

## Color Palette

- **Warm Mocha**: `#635A54` - Primary dark color replacing stark blacks/default slates for text and main elements.
- **Dusty Sage**: `#9FAFA1` - Soft, muted green serving as the primary accent color without overwhelming the quiet tone.
- **Linen**: `#FBFBF9` - Very light warm off-white, ideal for cards or elevated panel backgrounds.
- **Oatmeal**: `#F7F5F0` - The main background color, avoiding the harshness of pure white (`#ffffff`).

## Typography

- **Headers**: Lora Bold (or Roboto Slab) - Adds a touch of literary refinement and elegant contrast.
- **Body Text**: Nunito - A rounded, highly readable sans-serif that retains a friendly, organic feel.

## Best Used For

Personal portfolios, brand storytelling, editorial websites, artisan showcases, and any project that requires a calming, un-corporate atmosphere.
